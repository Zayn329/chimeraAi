# Chimera AI - UI/UX & Design Guidelines

## Visual Identity (Academic Brutalism)
- **NO GRADIENTS. NO GLOWING ORBS. NO PURPLE AI SLOP.**
- **Colors:** Pure White (#FFFFFF), Deep Black (#0F0F0F), subtle grays for borders.
- **Accent:** CRT Green (#00FF41) used ONLY for the Thought Log terminal and success states.
- **Typography:** `Inter` for general UI. `JetBrains Mono` or `Fira Code` for citations, code, and the Thought Log.
- **Components:** Strictly use `shadcn/ui` with Tailwind CSS. 1px borders, sharp corners (rounded-none or rounded-sm), high contrast.

## Landing Page Copy (Impact > Technical Blabber)
Do not use words like "Vector Databases" or "Cross-Encoders" on the landing page hero. Translate our tech into human impact.

**Hero Section:**
- **Headline:** AI that checks its own math.
- **Sub-headline:** Chimera is an autonomous academic engine that cross-references facts and proves its work. Built for students who can't afford to be wrong.
- **Primary CTA:** "Enter the Swarm →"

**Feature Grid (Translating the Tech):**
1. *Instead of "Multi-Agent Routing":* **A Team of Specialists, Not a Chatbot.** "Chimera routes your question to specialized experts. Syllabus questions go to the Tutor. Compliance questions go to the strict College Bureaucrat."
2. *Instead of "Multi-Hop Reasoning":* **Connects the Dots.** "It doesn't just search once. Chimera reads the syllabus, realizes it needs more depth, and dives into the textbook to build a complete answer."
3. *Instead of "Cross-Encoder Re-ranking":* **Mathematically Verified.** "Every answer is violently fact-checked against the original source text before you ever see it. Zero hallucinations. Total trust."
